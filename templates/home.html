What is Customer Churn?

Customer churn refers to customers who stop using a product or service. In a subscription-based model, understanding churn allows companies to retain more customers and increase revenue.

Our prediction tool uses machine learning algorithms to forecast whether a customer will likely stop using your service.

How Does It Work?

By inputting key metrics about customers such as age, account balance, activity status, number of products subscribed, and gender, our tool analyzes patterns and predicts churn probability.
